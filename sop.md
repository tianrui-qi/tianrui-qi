我的第一个research experience start from Dr. Ge Wang Lab 我在RPI大二的上学期加入了王革的实验室, focuing on foundation probabilistic and deep learning model development。To enrich the diversity of artidicial neurons in deep learning, a quadratic neurons was proposed by our lab previously. 我的主要工作是展示quadratic neuron相比于conventional neuron的优越性。To do so,我首先数学上derived了quadratic neural network的bacward propagation 的formulation,比较了conventional和quadratic neural network在inference和training,i.e., forward和backward propagation,上的区别,mathematically。其次,我用python的numpy库explicitly implement了conventional和quadratic neural network的training和prediction pipeline,instead of using directly call package like pytorch,as this is a training process for myself to really understand the implementation of fundational model. 最后,我demonstrate了single layer quadratic neural network rival conventional neural networks with hundreds of neurons in classifying simulated and real-world Gaussian mixture data, 展示了quadratic neuron的efficacy和efficiency。我的工作最后以第一作者身份在2022年9月发表在journal paper. 除此之外,我还继续探索了将quadratic neuron与active dendrites结合,develop quadratic dendritic network,探索quadratic neuron在adapting changing task contexts and continuous learning的可能性。在ge实验室期间,我开始将课上学习的algebra and computation实际的应用到research上,让我对这些知识的理解开始具像化,在computational上打下了坚实的基础。同时,在我的模型的验证阶段,我发现我缺少能够验证我模型application的数据,因为我的模型come up wtih statistical assumption instead of from real world problem,让我觉得是一个遗憾. 这让我未来的兴趣从pure computation model development转移到了apply computation to specific engineer problem. 

抱着apply computation to engineer problem的想法,结合我的摄影爱好,在2023年转学来GT之后,我加入了Dr. Shu Jia的Lab for Systems Biophotonics,focusing on optical imaging with deep learning. Me and my doctoral mentor aim to achieve volumatric sub-diffraction limit imaging using Light-field microscopy and single-molecule localization Microsocpy. To address the issue of volumetric localization data processing through the project, I engineered a scalable 3D U-Net pipeline based entirely on simulated data for volumetric lozalization, resolving sub-cellular stricture down to 20 nm. Second, I developed a patch-based strategy that divides field-of-view volumes into smaller sub-volumes, enables batch processing for efficient handling of datasets of different size and achieves a 100x speedup over conventional deterministic localization methods. In addition, I intergrated redundant cross-correlation algorihtm for drift correction with deep learning-based locolization pipeline, further improve the resolution of the final lozalized images. Our manuscript is in preparation and expected to submit at the end of 2024. At Jia Lab,I gained a solid understanding of theory in cellular imaging technique using fluorescence and super-resolution microsocpy, along with a overview of existing methods and background in this field. Also, I develop a fundamental understanding of the data I wil work with in practice, including how samples are prepared, roles of different components of the micrsocpy, how the micrsocope is controlled, and the data collection process. More important, I found optical imaging exactly fit my interest: 从具体的problem出发,design自己的硬件,并为这些硬件设计一套专用的算法。

不局限于学校的实验室内,拓展视野去看看工业界也同样重要,从2024年1月,我在Regeneron Genetics Center进行了为期30周的coop experience,focus on DNA sequence analysis with LLMs。因为WXS巨量数据的特性(100 million of sequences of DNA per sample), 使用全部WXS的数据进行sample wise之间的分析非常的困难。我在实习期间,第一,利用完全无监督流程,including pre-trained的BERT-based的LLMs和机器学习featurue selection,识别出WXS中最能体现sample wise difference 的position,并得到了每个WXS sample的vector representation。这些无监督识别出的position与已知的免疫相关的重要基因,如HLA-C, 的位置相吻合,并且我们通过直接使用vector representation进行immune system indicator prediction on 少部分 TCGA skin cancer数据集,展示了这些已经高度压缩的vector representation确实capture了 WXS中sample wise的重要信息。第二,为了能够在未来能够scale up到更大规模的WXS数据,translation to clinical trail,我optimizing parallel computing for high-performance computing (HPC) and enhancing file system efficiency through hashing。这scale up目前的pipeline能同时 handle/处理 1,000 WXS samples with 100 billion DNA sequences。在regeneron的这段经历,我所处理的WXS的数据量远远超过之前在实验室所处理的数据量,这让我在算法的设计,engineering,优化上有了更多的经验和思考。更重要的,在regeneron实习的这段经历比我前面两段经历都更加接近未来生产中应用,使得整个项目在推进的时候都需要考虑未来scale up的问题。我想要把在RGC学习到的translational的想法应用到optica imaging上。
